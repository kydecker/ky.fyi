**Ky Decker** (they/them) leads design and development at [Namesake](https://namesake.fyi), a non-profit they co-founded. Previously, they've built things at [Scribe](https://scribehow.com), [Cityblock](https://cityblock.com), [Dropbox](https://dropbox.com), and [Swiftype](https://swiftype.com). Their specialty is in building and maintaining design systems. You can [download Ky's resume here](/resume).

---

Most of my job titles have included the word "designer", but code is my medium and the browser is my favorite design tool. I believe that designing for the web means working _in the medium_ of the web—namely, HTML and CSS. Interactive media deserves interactive iteration.

I love working digitally, but when I need to think about gnarly problems, I take my sketchbook and my pen, I find a quiet place to sit, and I write.

In my free time, I play [piano](https://sheets.ky.fyi) and listen to city pop and noodle with synths and take [photos](https://glass.photo/kydecker) and bike around Brooklyn. I like reading about urbanism, transit, and queer history, and I tend to a small (but growing) apartment balcony garden.

I'm always open to chats and coffee. [Say hello!](mailto:hi@ky.fyi)
